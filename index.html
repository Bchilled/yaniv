How To Play

On your turn, discard one or more allowed cards, then draw one card. If your hand total is at or below the Yaniv limit, you can call Yaniv to end the round.

If someone matches or beats your total, you get an Assaf penalty. Scores add up across rounds. You can pick your favorite rules in Settings.
